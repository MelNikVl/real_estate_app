About this home

Beautiful, affordable three bedroom, two bath, two car garage brick home in an amazing neighborhood in Graham! This remarkable home was built in 1966 for a disabled Veteran according to the Veteran's Administration standards so it features uncommon amenities such as wide hallways, wide doorways and a chain-lift rail system in the ceiling from the primary bedroom to the fully accessible master ensuite. Bright and airy living room with tile flooring and a built-in bookshelf divider near the entrance. You'll love the open concept dining area and retro-style kitchen with a peninsula bar where your family and friends will congregate with ease! The dining area has a sliding glass door leading to a private concrete patio. The charming kitchen features a gas cooktop, built-in gas oven with broiler, built-in dishwasher, double sink, ample cabinetry, coffee bar and refrigerator which conveys to the Buyer. The washer and dryer are located in the garage and the washer and dryer will convey to the Buyer. The hall bathroom offers a tub-shower combo, oversized tile vanity with sink and toilet. Each bedroom is spacious and features ample closet space. One guest bedroom features carpet while the other has tile flooring. Easy maintenance tile flooring throughout most of the home. The primary bathroom has a wheelchair accessible sink, ample storage cabinets, a wheelchair accessible tile shower and a toilet. There is a small storage building for your lawn equipment and the backyard has a concrete walkway, chain link fence and wood privacy fence. Don't miss this wonderful home in a safe and quiet neighborhood. Some photos are Virtually Staged.
